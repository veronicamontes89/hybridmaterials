Welcome to the Hybrid Materials Group

Our team is located at the Wielkopolskie Centrum Zaawansowanych Technologii (WCZT) and the Faculty of Chemistry at Adam Mickiewicz University in Poznań. We are dedicated to exploring the design, synthesis, and application of hybrid materials for advanced technologies.

We focus on nanostructured materials that bridge the gap between molecular chemistry and functional devices. Our research spans supramolecular chemistry, energy storage, environmental remediation, and stimuli-responsive systems.

We invite you to explore our projects and meet our team, and we welcome collaborations across disciplines. If you’d like to learn more, feel free to browse our research sections or contact us directly.
