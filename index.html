Welcome to the Hybrid Materials Laboratory

We are an international research team established in 2016 at the Centre for Advanced Technologies at Adam Mickiewicz University (AMU). Our work focuses on the design, synthesis, and functional integration of hybrid and low-dimensional materials for applications in energy storage, environmental remediation, and advanced sensing.

We invite you to explore our projects and meet our team, and we are always open to collaborations across disciplines. If you are enthusiastic about cutting-edge materials science, we are actively looking for motivated researchers and students to join us.

Let's build the next generation of functional materials — together.
